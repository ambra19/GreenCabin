About Us

Mission

Empower Dutch farmers and rural landowners with an intuitive tool to assess, enhance, and sustain biodiversity on their land. Through actionable insights, community-driven collaboration, and nature-based solutions, we aim to make biodiversity restoration simple, impactful, and rewarding.

Mission

A future where every farmer and rural landowner in the Netherlands can actively monitor their land and take action to make it more biodiverse based on the insights they get from the app.
