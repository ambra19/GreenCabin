FAQ

How is the Biodiversity Score calculated?

The Biodiversity Score is based on land use, water quality, population density, spatial variation, and soil type. Your plot is analyzed against these factors to generate a biodiversity index.

How are Biodiversity Credits calculated?

Biodiversity Credits are determined by combining your Biodiversity Score and land area, processed through a model to assess ecological impact.

How does GreenCabin help reduce nitrogen on my land?

GreenCabin calculates nitrogen levels on your land and suggests practical steps to lower them, such as planting trees, improving soil health, reducing fertilizer use, and creating buffer zones to prevent nitrogen runoff.
